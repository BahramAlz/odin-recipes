Crispy baked cod with Rhode Island

Sometimes the simplest everyday meal is in the freezer. With crispy baked cod, boiled potatoes and a quick Rhode Island sauce, dinner is on the table in half an hour. If you have time, you can spice up the sauce with mustard and chili powder for more flavor.

Ingredients

  • 900 g potatoes (preferably delicate)
  • 2 pre-packaged crispy baked cod fillets (360 g each)
  • 2 tbsp chili sauce
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 1 pinch pepper
  • 1 cucumber
  • 1 tbsp oil (if frying the fish in a frying pan)
