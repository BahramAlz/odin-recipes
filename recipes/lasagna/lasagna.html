Classic Lasagna

Classic lasagna is a dish you never get tired of, right? With this recipe, your lasagna will be perfect with a soft and lovely texture and it also has a sweet and exemplary taste. The Parmesan cheese is the icing on the cake!

Ingredients

  • 2 gula lökar
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 500 g minced beef
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 4 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon dried rosemary
  • 390 g crushed tomatoes
  • 1 meat bouillon cube
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • 9 dried lasagna sheets

Sauce

  • 6 tablespoons butter (6 tablespoons equals about 90 g)
  • 6 tablespoons wheat flour
  • 10 dl milk
  • 2 dl grated parmesan

Steps

  1. Set the oven to 175°C.
  2. Peel and chop the onion and garlic. Fry the minced meat, onion and garlic in the oil in a frying pan. Add the tomato paste and fry it in the minced meat. Season with thyme and rosemary. Add the crushed tomatoes and stock cube and cook for about 10 minutes. Season with salt and pepper.
  3. Sauce: Melt the butter and stir in the flour. Dilute with the milk while whisking. Let the sauce cook for about 5 minutes, stirring occasionally. Stir in the Parmesan cheese and season with salt and pepper.
  4. Layer the sauce, lasagna sheets and minced meat sauce in an ovenproof dish (approx. 20 x 30 cm). Finish with the cheese sauce.
  5. Place the lasagna in the middle of the oven for about 40 minutes. (Or the time according to the packaging on the lasagna sheets.)
  6. To serve: Serve the lasagna with salad.
